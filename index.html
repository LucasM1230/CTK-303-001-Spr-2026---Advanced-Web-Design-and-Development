Monk Specializations

A simple showcase of the three Monk specializations in World of Warcraft.

Brewmaster — Tank

Brewmaster

A sturdy brawler who uses unpredictable movement and mystical brews to avoid damage and protect allies.

Mistweaver — Healer

Mistweaver

A healer who masters the art of life energy, guided by the wisdom of the Jade Serpent.

Windwalker — Damage

Windwalker

A martial artist who overwhelms enemies with speed, pressure, and nonstop strikes.

Signature Ability

Stagger

You shrug off attacks, delaying a portion of Physical damage based on your Agility instead of taking it over 10 sec. Affects magical attacks at reduced effectiveness. Gain up to 15% Mastery based on your current level of Stagger.

Signature Ability

Renewing Mist

Surrounds the target with healing mists, restoring health over 22 sec. If Renewing Mist heals a target past maximum health, it will travel to another injured ally within range. Maximum of 3 charges.

Signature Ability

Fists of Fury

Pummels all targets in front of you, dealing damage over 4 sec. Deals primary damage to your main target and reduced damage to nearby enemies. Can be channeled while moving.
