Billboard #1 on My Birthday

This page looks at the Billboard Hot 100 song that was #1 during the week I was born. I was born on December 30, 1998, and the top song that week was “I’m Your Angel” by R. Kelly & Céline Dion. I wanted to turn that into a small time capsule tied to music from that moment.

Song
I’m Your Angel
R. Kelly & Céline Dion
Chart week: Week of Dec 26, 1998
Video
Listen to the song on YouTube
Lyric excerpt
I'll be your cloud up in the sky... I'll be your shoulder when you cry... I'll hear your voices when you call me... I am your angel

The song focuses on reassurance and being there for someone when things feel overwhelming. A lot of the lyrics are about trust, belief, and offering support, which gives the song a calm, emotional tone.
